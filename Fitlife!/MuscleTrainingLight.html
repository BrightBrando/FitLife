Light exercises for beginners!

Bodyweight Squats

Strengthens your legs and glutes while improving balance and everyday movement.

Push-ups

Builds upper-body strength in your chest, shoulders, and arms in a beginner-friendly way.

Superman

A back exercise where you lie face down and lift your arms, chest, and legs off the ground at the same time. It strengthens the lower back, glutes, and core while improving posture and stability.

Plank

Strengthens your core to enhance stability, posture, and overall body control.
