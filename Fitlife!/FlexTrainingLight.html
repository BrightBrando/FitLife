GENTLE MOBILITY FOR BEGINNERS

Spinal Twist Stretch

Improve spinal mobility and relieve lower back tension by rotating the upper body while keeping hips stable.

Prone Quad Stretch

Lie face down and gently pull your foot toward your glutes to improve flexibility in the front of the thigh.

Shoulder Stretch

Bring one arm across your body to reduce upper body stiffness and improve range of motion in the shoulders.

Cat-Cow Stretch

Cycle between arching and rounding your back on all fours to relieve back tension and wake up the spine.
