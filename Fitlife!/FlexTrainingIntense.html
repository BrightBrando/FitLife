Extreme exercises for those who want to push their limits and achieve maximum muscle growth!

Spinal Twist Stretch

A stretch where you rotate your upper body to one side while keeping your hips stable. It helps improve spinal mobility and relieves tension in the lower back and core.

Prone Quad Stretch

A stretch where you lie face down and pull one foot toward your glutes while keeping your hips on the ground. It targets the quadriceps and helps improve flexibility in the front of the thigh.

Shoulder Stretch

A stretch where you bring one arm across your body and hold it with the opposite hand. It helps improve shoulder mobility and reduces upper body stiffness.

Cat-Cow Stretch

A controlled movement where you alternate between arching and rounding your back while on all fours. It improves spinal flexibility and helps relieve tension in the back.
