WEIGHT RANGE NUTRITION GUIDE

50-60 kg

Macros
  • Protein: 80–130g
  • Carbs: 150–300g
  • Fats: 25–60g
  • Cal: +300–500 Surplus
Foods
  • Chicken, Eggs, Fish
  • Rice, Bread, Oats
  • Peanut Butter
Schedule
  • 3–5 meals daily
  • Protein every meal
  • Pre/Post workout fuel
Key rule: If your weight isn’t going up, eat more!
